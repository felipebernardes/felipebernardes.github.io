  9. October 2016

    • organized the 1st CodePen Meetup Brazil

      #event

      OMG! I can't believe I could be part of the organization for the first CodePen Meetup Brazil ever - and also the first one of Latin America!

      The event was 5 hours long. Five speakers, three months and more than 40 hours spent by the organization (which had more than 10 people), a crowd of 78 incredible people, coffee break and networking 40 minutes long, the comfortable structure of Fatec Sorocaba and lots of gifts from Codepen and also from our sponsors.

      Most of all, I felt everyone that went left inspired to create more using web technologies. This, IMO, is the greatest feeling a organizer can experience after a sucessful event.

      A friend of mine, André Christian, wrote a review about the event @ SorocabaCSS blog.

    • organized 23th Semana de Tecnologia @ Fatec Sorocaba college

    • presented 'start a local community' talk @ 23th Semana de Tecnologia

  10. September 2016

    • participated on HackZurich

      #hackathon

      I'm very thankful to GS1 Brazil and to my team (@celsowo, @gabrieljacquier & @mikelima) for competing on HackZurich, the biggest EU hackathon.

      We've built Abroad (demo | GitHub), an app for sending indoor warning messages & locating people in case of natural disasters. It was based on Cisco Meraki access point. I even had the privilege to present our solution to Cisco Team!

      Unfortunately, we didn't win the hackathon, but it was indeed one of the most richiest experiences in my life - HackZurich totally redefined my mindset and perspective of technology innovation.

  11. August 2016

    • won GS1 Brazil hackathon

      #hackathon

      I was 1/4 (alongside @celsowo, @gabrieljacquier & @mikelima) of the 1st place winner of GS1 Brazil hackathon, Tegra team!

      That was the 4th hackathon I had participated on, but the best organized in terms of mindset, venue, challenges & menthorship.

      We've created PagFarma, an app for saving money when buying montlhy medicines. For the costumer perspective, this was an entire new way to quickly save money buying remedies. For the drugstores, a new selling channel for achieving more profit.

      Besides developing the front end, I was responsible for the pitch - I remember to practice it more than 3h straight (~36 times).

      I didn't expect us to win, specially being among a lot of creative projects, but we made it. And it made me realize that I could win and achieve more in my career and life.
